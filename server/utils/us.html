این Introduction to International Marriage

International marriage means marriage among people from different countries. This type of marriage can have many benefits for people. Among these benefits are the possibility of obtaining a permanent residence or a spouse visa, which can be accessed in various countries, including Türkiye, through marriage to a person from that country.
